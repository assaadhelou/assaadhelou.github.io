About

I am a Mechanical Engineering PhD candidate at Southern Methodist University (SMU, Dallas, TX) with an interest in Thermal/Fluids sciences and research in thermal characterization and management of microelectronic devices. Skilled in numerical analysis (Matlab, LabVIEW, Mathematica and ANSYS) and experimental design using optical, thermal, and electronic systems (LabVIEW, NI DAQ). Experienced researcher, teaching assistant and lab instructor with knowledge of thermal analysis and computational methods and modeling with interdisciplinary knowledge of solid state physics and semiconductor devices. Obtained cleanroom training and gained experience in electronic microfabrication.
